About

Nestled in the tranquil village of Kaipuzha, Padipura is a charming independent house located just off the Neendoor–Kottayam road and a short walk from the Kaipuzha petrol station. The property sits between the towns of Kottayam (12 km) and Ettumanoor (8 km), making it an ideal base for exploring the region while enjoying village serenity.

The home features three comfortable bedrooms – two air‑conditioned and one non‑air‑conditioned – along with three full bathrooms. A spacious living room, dining area, and fully‑equipped kitchen provide everything you need for a relaxing stay. Wide verandas at the front and back of the house invite you to enjoy Kerala’s gentle breeze, while the large, open yard can accommodate small gatherings or events.

Fully furnished and able to sleep up to eight guests, Padipura offers the warmth of a family home with the conveniences of modern living. The property is ideally suited for expatriate families looking for a peaceful retreat without straying too far from town amenities.
